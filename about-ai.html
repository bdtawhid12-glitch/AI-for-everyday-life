Let's know about AI

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the study and design of systems that can perform tasks that usually require human intelligence — like understanding language, recognizing images, making decisions, or translating text.

Everyday examples

  • Smartphone assistants (Siri, Google Assistant)
  • Recommendation systems (YouTube, Spotify)
  • Autocorrect and translation tools
  • Navigation and traffic predictions

How it works (brief)

At a high level, many modern AIs learn from data. They train on examples, discover patterns, and then make predictions or produce outputs based on those patterns.
